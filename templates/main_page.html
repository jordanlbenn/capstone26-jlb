Encryption Decision and Implementation System

Educational tool for exploring encryption methods

Welcome to my web-based encryption decision and implementation system! This platform is designed to help users understand the importance of encryption, explore different encryption methods, and see how they can be applied in real-world scenarios. Whether you're a student, educator, or just curious about encryption, this tool provides an interactive way to learn and experiment with various encryption techniques.

To get started, you can take a quiz that will assess your needs and recommend an appropriate encryption method. Alternatively, you can skip the quiz and directly apply a simple encryption method to see how it works.
